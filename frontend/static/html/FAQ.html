Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on "Create Account". You have to enter a username, your e-mail address and your name, as well as a password.

To create a shootout, please click on "Compare Pictures". You can then select "Create shootout" on the bottom left of the page.

When you've selected a shootout, two pictures will appear on your screen.
Click on the picture you like better to select it as a favorite. Once you have selected your favourite, the other picture will be replaced by another picture of the shootout.

When you create a shootout, all visitors can view it, but only other klicks users can participate in it.

If you want to find someone else's shootout, just search for the name of the shootout in the search bar on the top left of the page.

Your pictures belong to you only. Klicks does not forward any pictures to our partners or other websites.

To upload pictures that you can use to fill a shootout, go to your profile and klick on the "Upload Picture" button.

Shootouts can contain as many pictures as you want.
